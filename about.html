About ExpenseAI

ExpenseAI is a smart expense and budget tracking website designed to help users take control of their finances. Our goal is to make money management simple, visual, and intelligent.

The platform allows users to record their daily expenses, categorize spending, and visualize their financial habits through interactive charts and reports.

Using built-in AI-based suggestions, ExpenseAI analyzes spending trends and offers practical tips to save more and spend wisely.

This project was developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, focusing on a clean design, responsive layout, and efficient local data storage.
